## What Is This?

A growth mindset for engineering is the belief that abilities and intelligence can be developed through hard work, dedication, and persistence. 

Imagine you're learning to cook. At first, you might struggle to make a simple meal, but with practice and patience, you can become a skilled chef. This mindset is essential for engineers because it allows them to approach challenges with confidence and resilience.
